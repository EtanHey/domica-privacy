Privacy Policy for Domica

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Domica ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Domica collects minimal information to provide our service:

  • Location Data: We temporarily access your device location to center the map view and show nearby rental properties. Your location is not stored on our servers.
  • User Preferences: Your saved favorites, search filters, and app settings are stored locally on your device only.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Display your current location on the map
  • Show rental properties near you
  • Remember your app preferences and favorites

We do not:

  • Store your location on our servers
  • Share your location with third parties
  • Track your movements or location history
  • Sell any of your data

4. Data Storage and Security

Property listings displayed in the app are stored in our database (powered by Supabase) with industry-standard security measures including row-level security policies.

Your personal preferences (favorites, filters, language settings) are stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms.

5. Third-Party Services

Domica uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Mapbox: Provides map tiles and display functionality. Mapbox may collect anonymous usage data. See Mapbox Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Hosts our property database with secure access controls. See Supabase Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Domica is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Location Permissions: You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings → Domica → Location
  • Local Data: You can delete all saved favorites and preferences by uninstalling the app
  • Data Deletion: Contact us to request deletion of any data associated with your account

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
